Description:

An excellent adventure fantasy with elements of mystical action about the coexistence of humans and supernatural beings, as well as a magical order that opposes curses.

According to the plot, high school student Yuji Itadori has been distinguished by his shyness and athleticism since childhood. His best friend, his grandfather, fell seriously ill, so Yuji always tries to be closer to the old man. The grandfather shares various legends with his grandson, prompting Yuji to join a mysterious occult club. Unexpectedly, the boy finds himself at the center of a magical confrontation that unfolds around collecting pieces of a powerful demon that can grant their owner unlimited power...
